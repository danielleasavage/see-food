SeeFood

Where Does Your Food Come From?

The map above displays the total number of tons of food & feed each country has produced for the year 2013. Using the drop down menu, one can select a region to get a closer up, isolated view. Hovering over a country shows the exact aggregate tonnage.

A Timeline Comparison

Looking at Food Production over the Years

The time series plot above show how each region’s annual food & feed production in tons has evolved from 1961 to 2013. Hovering over the trend line on each graph shows the exact year/tonnage coordinates. On this region overview page, y-axes have been scaled to be the same for more intuitive comparison between regions. Click on the region name above any graph to see a breakdown of every country in that region.

Regional Specialties

Breakdown of Food Groups

The bar charts below above the categorical breakdown of each region’s food & feed production in tons for year 2013. Hovering over bars shows the exact amount of tonnage in each category. On this region overview page, y-axes have been scaled to be the same for more intuitive comparison between regions. Click on the region name above any graph to see a breakdown of every country in that region.
